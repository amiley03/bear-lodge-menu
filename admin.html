How to Edit the Menu

  1. Click a category tab below to see its items
  2. Click "Edit" to modify an item, or "Add New Item" to create one
  3. Click "Save to Cloud" (top right) to save your changes
  4. Refresh the menu page to see your updates!

Tip: For images, use URLs from Unsplash (free). Search for the food at unsplash.com, right-click the image, and copy the image URL.
